Teams Squad Presents
DAY 1 | WEDNESDAY, APRIL 22
Keynote
9:00 AM · All Hands · Andrew Lee & Jason Rickard
State of the Union. Vision for H2. The North Star and the purpose of this offsite. This is not a status update—it is a declaration of intent. How we are changing the way we work, pushing boundaries, doing away with legacy processes, and taking full ownership of our products and our craft.
Predictive Index
9:30 AM · Workshop · Leah Press & Ali Love
In this hands-on activity, we'll use our PI results to map out how our squad naturally works. We'll identify where our styles click and where they might clash based on our different needs for things like speed, data, and social interaction. It's a quick way to learn how to adjust your own approach to make collaborating with your teammates much easier.
Ways of Working (with AI)
10:00 AM · Panel · P/D/E + PMM Leads
Candid, cross-functional conversation about what AI means for every role in the room. What used to be bottlenecks. What are the bottlenecks now? The truth: we are all becoming generalists. The old gatekeeping is gone. The new skill is directing AI with precision and taste. How are people using AI in their specific area of responsibility? Where are the overlapping areas within all of our roles? Open Q&A.
☕ Break · 11:00 AM
AI Enablement Session
11:15 AM · Hands-On Lab · Cursor Setup + Role-Tailored Build Exercises
Close the gap between understanding AI conceptually and actually using it to produce work. Every single person leaves with a working setup and at least one completed exercise. No one walks away saying 'that was interesting' without having built or queried something real.
🍽 Lunch · 12:00 PM
Know Your Customer
12:45 PM · 45 min · Kaelin Allmendinger
This session introduces Product Marketing Management (PMM)—who we are, what we do, and how we partner across teams to drive product success. We'll cover PMM's role in connecting market insight, customer needs, and product strategy to ensure we're building solutions that resonate and win. From there, we'll shift into "Know Your Customer," focusing on building a clear, shared understanding of who we're designing for. We'll explore key customer segments, their needs, pain points, and how these insights should inform product decisions.
Investing for Scale
1:30 PM · 30 min · Catherine Hamet / Kaelin
As we continue to deliver for customers and drive near-term revenue, this session focuses on strengthening how we prioritize product investments for scale. We'll introduce a more disciplined, dual-lens approach to decision-making—evaluating both customer and market value alongside effort and feasibility—to better assess tradeoffs, prioritize work with repeatable impact, and balance short-term delivery with long-term differentiation.
Hackathon Kickoff
2:00 PM · Brainstorm / Launch
Launch the hackathon with enough energy, clarity, and structure that teams can start immediately without spinning. Theme, rules, judging criteria, and team composition need to be crisp.
